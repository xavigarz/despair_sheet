Backgrounds

Your Background is your life. Well, your previous life. It helps you to understand what your character did until they got their Mark, what they know, what they are good at, what possible skills and feats they can have.

A Background gives your character a boost in Attributes, some Skills (with specialities), a Feat and one additional talent.

Mark

Marks are the results of being exposed to the influence of the Realms Beyond. In most of the cases, the interaction with those phenomena is fatal for a normal human. However, the few unfortunate that survive an encounter with that horror will develop certain size effects. They have been marked.

Some marks are visible: for example, someone marked by Mark of Destruction will show burned skin or scars, while a person bearing the Mark of Corruption will have pustulent or other infectious things.

Other marks are more subtle, they leave a mark in one's soul.
